Session Types

  • Headshot

    • Incluses a 30min indoor session at the studio with one-two outfit changes. Perfect for professional or commercial work!

  • Family Session

    • Inclues a 1 or 2 hour session, indoors or outdoors. You get to pick the location and can coordinate one-two outfits. Animals are welcome and incouraged!

  • Weddings/Engagement

    • These sessions can be booked together or seperately. Includes a 1 hour engagement session indoors or outdoors, multiple outfits and or locations. Weddings are booked by the day and can include other optional packages.

  • Outdoor Session

    • Wether you like to ski, fish, hike, or climb, there is an activity you can do. These photos will help capture you in what you love to do, and can be very vercital.

  • Sports

    • Session includes in-game shots as well as studio session pictures of your choice.

  • Events

    • Photos of the event set up, small detailed items, group photos, and much more. An easy way to document the whole event and pictures you will be able to send to guests.
