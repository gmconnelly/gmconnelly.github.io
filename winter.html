Winter Facts

Many people enjoy drinking coffee and cocoa in the winter. The nights of the winter are longer than a day, so you would think you would get more sleep, however school ruins that. Many animals migrate from one place to other to reach the warmer areas, and some hybernate for the entire season, something many humans get jealous of.
